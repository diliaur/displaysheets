Southeast Asia Related Scholarships

This is a list of scholarships which may be applicable or related to study in or of Southeast Asia. We try to keep it up to date, but due to staffing constraints, some things may get past us. Please alert us of any broken links or other issues at cseasweb@hawaii.edu. Because this is a curated list (these awards, unless otherwise stated, are not administered by CSEAS), please see the contact point or website for more information on specific awards. You can also examine the spreadsheet itself if you prefer a tabular data format.

Key

This key defines some terminology used in the scholarship descriptions.
  • Fund source: internal/external - whether the source is UH MƒÅnoa or an external entity.
  • Length: short term/long term/n/a - period of time of the award if stated. Time periods mostly apply to workshop and professional devlopment type awards
  • Grade level: undergraduate/graduate/faculty/professional
  • Place of Study - country or countries where you will be able to apply the award
  • SEA Country Focus - some awards are specific to a certain country or countries in ASEAN
  • Discipline Focus - which fields/disciplines are covered by the award
  • Citizenship - citizenship requirement of the applicant
