About this Resource

This is a list of scholarships which may be applicable or related to study in or of Southeast Asia. This list is curated by the Center for Southeast Asian Studies, University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa. Because this is a curated list (these awards, unless otherwise stated, are not administered by CSEAS), please see the contact point or website for more information on specific awards.

We try to keep it up to date but some things may get past us. Please alert us of any broken links or other issues at cseasweb@hawaii.edu.

Key

This key defines some terminology used in the scholarship descriptions.
  • Fund source: internal/external - whether the source is UH Mānoa (internal) or an external entity.
  • Length: short term/long term/n/a - period of time of the award if stated. Time periods mostly apply to workshop and professional devlopment type awards
  • Grade level: undergraduate/graduate/faculty/professional
  • Place of Study - country or countries where you will be able to apply the award
  • SEA Country Focus - some awards are specific to study in/of a certain country or countries in SEA
  • Discipline Focus - which fields/disciplines are covered by the award
  • Citizenship - citizenship requirement of the applicant
